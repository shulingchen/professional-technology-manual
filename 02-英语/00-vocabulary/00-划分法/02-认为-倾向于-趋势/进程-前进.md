## progression
```
n. 进程, 前进, 连续, （进入另一阶段的）发展

例句

The Department of Disease Control and Prevention immediately took strict measures to prevent the rapid progression of the disease.

疾病防控部门立即采取了严格的措施，防止疾病的迅速发展。
A progression of disagreements has led to war between those nations.

一连串的不和导致那些国家之间爆发了战争.
```
