## compliance
```
n. 遵从, 顺从, 服从

例句

The company claimed that all their production process were in compliance with food laws.

该公司声称他们所有的生产过程都符合食品法规。
```
## submissive
```
adj. 唯命是从的, 顺从的, 驯服的, 听话的

例句

Most kindergarten teachers want the children to be submissive.

大多数幼儿园教师都想让小孩子们顺从听话。
He is a humble and submissive servant.

他是个恭顺的仆人。
```
